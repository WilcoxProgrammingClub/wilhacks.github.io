About The Event

WilHacks 3.0 is an online, 27 hour hackathon run by Wilcox High School's Programming Club. Work in teams of up to four to create unique projects and win cool prizes! We encourage and welcome any high school student to participate, regardless of programming experience. More details will be posted soon.

F.A.Q

  • What is the goal of a hackathon?

    The goal of a hackathon is to create fully functioning software by the end of the event. The software can be a website, an app, a game, and so on.

  • Are there any prerequisites or required skills?

    While coding and programming is a huge plus, teams will also need people with excellent ideas and strong presenting skills.

  • Who can take part in the hackathon?

    The hackathon is open to any high school students or home schoolers in the equivalent grade.

  • How will teams work?

    Teams can be between 1-4 people. There will also be an opportunity to meet new people and form teams during the hackathon.

  • How much does it cost?

    The hackathon is completely free to attend!

  • Are there prizes?

    There are various prizes for various categories! We will announce the exact prizes closer to the date of the hackathon.

  • Who will be judging?

    The judges will be announced closer to the date of the hackathon.
